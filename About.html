Welcome to Agbonran School Of Science, Ede, Osun State.

We are dedicated to providing top-quality education and shaping future leaders. With a strong focus on academic excellence, character development, and innovation, we aim to inspire every student to reach their full potential.

About Us

Science Secondary School is dedicated to fostering a love for science and innovation in our students. We aim to create future leaders who will shape the world with their knowledge and skills.

Our Mission

To provide a well-rounded education that emphasizes scientific exploration and critical thinking. We empower students to excel academically and personally, preparing them for the challenges of the future.

Our Vision

To be a beacon of excellence in science education, inspiring curiosity and innovation in students from all backgrounds.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We foster honesty and strong moral principles in every student.

Excellence

We encourage the pursuit of academic and personal excellence.

Teamwork

Collaboration and mutual support are at the heart of what we do.

Creativity

We inspire innovation and creative thinking in all our students.
